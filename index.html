ABOUT THE EVENT

DAY ONE

Masterclass session

The masterclass session will begin the main events of the fashion week. the master class session will be an open event where all OAU student can attend free of charge. However, the event will be accessed only through pre-registration (for planning purposes).

View details

DAY TWO

Runway & Exhibition

The Runway show is the most important day in the series of events for the week. It will feature design collections from fashion creatives amongst OAU students and other local designers. The designers will also get an opportunity to showcase their collection for sale in the exhibition sales.

View details

DAY THREE

Dinner and Awards

The event will host designers and special guests to appreciate and celebrate those who have been outstanding. The most creative designers; the most oustanding models; the most creative stylists; and other people who have contributed immensely to the success of the event will all be recognised.

View details

ABOUT OAUFW.

The OAU Fashion week maiden edition will be an opportunity to celebrate students and local creatives. it will also be as an avenue to contribute to the growth of made in Nigeria fashion products.
